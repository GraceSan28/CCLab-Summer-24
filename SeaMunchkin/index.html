||Sea Munchkin||

Scientific Name:

[ MARINUS GLUTTOPHAGUS ]

Discovered by / at:

Grace San, 11:11

Family:

One of its kind but seems to be a descendent of Ditto from the Pokemon Multiverse.

Habitat:

Crystal clear and bubbly bodies of water with a lot of sunshine.

Appearance:

The SeaMunchkin's appearance transforms depending on its hunger level/mood. Usually a pale moody blob when hungry, a happy darker blob while eating, and only reveals its true form when its belly is satisfied; a true munch.

Key Characteristics:

  • Very moody.
  • Greedy: only eats the FRESHEST man-made food.
  • Transforms as it grows
  • Expressionist; WILL be mad if not fed
  • ...

What we know so far about [ Sea Munchkin ]? The Sea Munchkin is the only one of its kind discovered to date and has been given the scientific name Marinus Gluttophagus by its discoverer, Grace San, after she observed Munchkin's immaculate appetite. While not fully known how or where the Munchkin spawned from, it is believed that the creature is a variant of descendant of the Pokemon "Ditto" from the Pokemon Multiverse due to its ability to shapeshift. Its ability to express its emotions also has proven it to be a highly intelligent creature that can interact and co-exist with humans. Some have brought up the ideas of domesticating the Munchkin and keeping it as a pet; especially because it only seems to eat man-made food.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature

Make sure to have enough food for the Munchkin or it'll make sure you know its not happy! Munchkin's whole life purpose is to munch, munch, and munch. Continue feeding the creature until it decides to show you its true form!
